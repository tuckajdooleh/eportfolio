Quarantine Simulator 3D Game

Play Online ( Slow, Buggy )

Download for Windows ( Faster )

I created this for my 3D Game Programming final project. I used Godot, and Blender for the game engine and the 3D modeling and animation. I exported this as a windows game and an HTML game.

The goal is to get to the shopping area ( located by the giant green light in the sky ), grab 10 bananas, breads, and toilet papers, and get back home without getting infected. Also this game is annoyingly hard and should definitely be made easier.

There's the intro scene, city level, shopping level, and ending scene.

Blender has an Open Street Map addon that lets you select a portion of a map and generate the 3D buildings for that area. I used this to make a 3D model of Indianapolis. I still had to manually fine tune the model and texture everything myself. I used google maps satellite imagery for the ground, but that didn’t look very well so I should have used my own textures. I also used a couple different free textures for the buildings, roads, and the shopping level.

The enemies just walk in random directions for random times and wait a random amount of time. They are also randomly assigned “infected” states. They can infect you, or other enemies if you get close to them. The goal is to avoid them. They also dynamically despawn when you get far away from them, and respawn around you as you walk around.

I created the cars which drive in a straight line wherever I placed them and will collide with buildings and the player. When the player gets hit it will go into a death cam scene showing the player getting hit and turning into a ragdoll. You then have the option to quit or start over.

Once you get to the green light and walk in, the shopping level will start. You pick up items by looking at them and left clicking. Once you have 10 of each you can leave. Then you just navigate back to your house and the game is over.
